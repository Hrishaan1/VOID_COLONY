  • Added Credits currency system
  • Colonizing, farms & fuel gens now cost credits
  • Food Market with fluctuating prices — check before selling

  • Full solar system layout with named star at center
  • Planets orbit the star in real time at varying speeds
  • Moons orbit planets — colonizable with 1 slot each

  • Orbital co-movement: ship drifts with nearby planets
  • Planet collision physics with bounce
